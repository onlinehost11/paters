About Paters Group

Discover our mission, values, and the people behind the ministry

Our History

Paters Group was established in 1973 as a visionary response to the need for authentic Christian community and spiritual growth. What began as a small prayer circle has blossomed into a vibrant ministry touching hundreds of lives across our region.

Over the past five decades, our ministry has remained steadfast in our commitment to exalt Christ, equip believers, and empower communities with the transformative message of the Gospel. Through the faithfulness of God and dedication of our members, Paters Group has grown into a beacon of hope and spiritual excellence.

Our Mission

To create a community of authentic believers who worship God wholeheartedly, grow in Christ-likeness, serve their communities with excellence, and impact the world for the Kingdom of God.

Core Values

Christ-Centered

Jesus Christ is the center of all we do. We believe in His resurrection power and transformative grace.

Community

We believe in the power of authentic relationships, mutual support, and genuine Christian fellowship.

Biblical Foundation

God's Word is our guide. We believe in the authority and relevance of Scripture for today's challenges.

Excellence

We pursue excellence in all endeavors as an act of worship and service to our Lord.

Service

We are called to serve with compassion, reaching the marginalized and lifting the downtrodden.

Spiritual Growth

We are committed to continuous spiritual development and discipleship at every level.
