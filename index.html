About Paters Group

Paters Group is a vibrant Christian ministry dedicated to fostering fellowship, spiritual growth, and Christ-centered community. We believe in the power of small groups to transform lives and strengthen faith through authentic relationships and God's Word.

Our mission is to create a welcoming space where believers come together to worship, learn, serve, and grow closer to God and each other. Through our various cells and activities, we aim to be a beacon of hope and light in our community.
